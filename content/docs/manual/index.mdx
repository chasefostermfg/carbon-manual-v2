This is the written guide to **Carbon** — the read-it-yourself companion to
[Carbon Academy](https://learn.carbon.ms). It's made for people who'd rather read than
watch a video. Every page is short, uses real screenshots, and shows you **exactly where
to click**.

## Carbon is made of Modules

The easiest way to picture Carbon: it's split into **Modules** — one for each part of your
business. Selling lives in **Sales**. Buying lives in **Purchasing**. Making things lives in
**Production**. And so on.

When you log in, you land on the **home screen**. Every tile here is a Module — click one to
start working in it.
